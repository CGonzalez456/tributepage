Chadwick Boseman

Chadwick Boseman studied at two different colleges. The first being Howard University in Washington D.C. The second being Balliol College, Oxford in England.

Chadwick Boseman didn't initially audition for the Role of T'challa in the movie Black Panther. He actually auditioned for Drax in the movie Guardians of the Galaxy but, that role was given to Dave Bautista.

Chadwick Boseman was raised by his parents Carolyn Boseman and Leroy Boseman.

Chadwick Boseman was inspired to become an actor by Phylicia Rashad.

Chadwick Boseman grew up in Anderson, South California.
